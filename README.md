# GIFDistributor

> Upload once, publish everywhere. Your GIFs on GIPHY, Tenor, and every chat app - in minutes.

## What You Can Do

- **Publish everywhere at once** - Upload a GIF and it lands on GIPHY, Tenor, and your own CDN automatically
- **One link works everywhere** - Get a short link that auto-embeds perfectly in Slack, Discord, Teams, and Twitter
- **Find your GIFs in any picker** - Search for your own GIFs in Discord's or Slack's built-in GIF picker
- **Watch them spread** - Real-time analytics show views, shares, and engagement across every platform
- **Stay safe for work** - AI moderation ensures your uploads meet platform requirements before they go live
- **Send directly to Discord** - Push GIFs straight to Discord channels without leaving the app

Upload a GIF. Add a title and tags. Hit publish.
